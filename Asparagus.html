Asparagus Soup

Servings: 3 bowls

Directions

  1. Place asparagus onions & 1/2 cup vegitable broth in a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Lower heat and simmer until tender
  3. Melt butter in same pot, stir in flour, brown and cook 2 min.
  5. Add remainder of beans
  6. Combine cornstarch with water
  7. Add lemon juice & cornstarch to soup, cook until thickend
  8. Serve in sourdough bread bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 celery ribs, chopped
  • 1/4 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 chicken bolilon cubes
  • 1 & 1/4 cups boiling water
  • 2 cans black beans, undrained
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 & 1/2 Tbs corn starch
  • 1 & 1/2 Tbs water
