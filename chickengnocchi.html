Chicken & Gnocchi Soup

Servings: 5 bowls

Directions

  1. Melt butter into EVOO in a large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add onion, celery, garlic & cook
  3. Whisk in flour and cook 1 min
  4. Whisk in half and half and simmer until thickened
  5. Stir in 1/2 tsp salt, thyme, parsley, carrots, spinach, chick & gnocchi
  6. Simmer until heated through

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbs butter
  • 1 Tbs Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO)
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup celery (diced)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 quart half & half
  • 32 ounces chicken broth
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp parsely
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup spinach leaves, chopped
  • rotisserie chciken
  • 1 package (16 oz) gnocchi
